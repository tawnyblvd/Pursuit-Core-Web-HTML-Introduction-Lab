Arroz con Dulce

Description

I make this rice pudding every year during the holidays. My dad usually makes coquito at the same time so we have a festive dessert and drink to pair! You'll find I am more liberal with certain ingredients than a similar recipe I'll link to below.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 cups water for the spices
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 4 cinnamon sticks
  • 6 whole cloves
  • 1-1in. piece of ginger, peeled and sliced
  • 1 can coconut milk -I recommend grabbing one if possible from a Latin aisle as Thai brands tend to be a little thicker and less sweet. We want sweet for this recipe!
  • 1 cup water for soaking and cooking the rice
  • 1 cup short grain rice ideally, though medium grain is ok too
  • 1/4 cup currants or raisins - some people like more, but I am not a huge fan so I put less. Feel free to toss more in to the mix!
  • 1/4 cup coconut flakes
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • ground cinnamon - leave til the end!
